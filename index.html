Exploring the Beauty of Pokhara, Nepal

Pokhara is one of the most beautiful cities in Nepal. Surrounded by majestic mountains and serene lakes, it offers breathtaking views and peaceful moments. This was one of the best trips I've ever had.



Top 5 Things I Did in Pokhara

  • Boating on Phewa Lake
  • Hiking to the World Peace Pagoda
  • Watching sunrise at Sarangkot
  • Exploring caves like Mahendra Gufa
  • Tasting local Nepali food

My Travel Plan

  1. Book bus tickets
  2. Pack essentials
  3. Reach Pokhara in the morning
  4. Explore city and relax
